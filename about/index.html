About

Durango Ruby School was started in 2014 by a small group of passionate Ruby developers to spread the Ruby gospel and inspire Rails development. Co-founders Aaron Renner and Tristan Rubadeau formulated the idea at a Durango Coders event about the Jekyll blogging platform. They decided that we needed more Ruby and Rails developers in Durango and the best way to do it would be through a didactic effort to share the collective knowledge and experience of local developers. Aaron immediately jumped in head first, compiling a curriculum to engage programmers at all levels. Together with the other instructors, he used the Thoughtbot Rails tutorials to guide the design and content of the course.

Durango Ruby School is completely free and consists of structured weekly classes taught at the SCAPE offices.
